Who is Twin Eye Photography

Twin Eye Photography has been capturing weddings since 2017 as well as shooting many corporate events in Colombo, Sri Lanka.

Navod Samarawithana (Professional Sri Lankan Photographer) is the founder of Twin Eye Photography & Live Motion Productions. He discovered photography soon after Passed out from Secondary Education. We are represented by a dedicated and talented team of individuals with professional working experience in photography. Capturing the most important moments of your life is what we do best. Whether you are sharing your ceremony vows, gathered for a family reunion, or documenting an important business event, we are committed to a personable and enjoyable experience with you. We will be there with you every step of the way to guarantee your special moments are captured for all time.

We specialize in wedding photography sri lanka, Event Photography and Fashion Photography. We make being photographed a comfortable experience so that you have images to cherish for lifetime. While photography continues to change in its style and technology, we stay focused on what really matters. Through the years, we’re learned that there isn’t just one solution that works. That’s why we use a combination of photography styles and technology to capture you and your moments.

You might say we use a hybrid approach.
